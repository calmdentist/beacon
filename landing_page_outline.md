## 2. Problem Section

### Section Header

### 3 short points
- LLMs help you explore ideas deeply, but those explorations usually die in private threads
- Posting to social media is noisy, performative, and depends on followers
- The people who could actually help are often invisible to you

### One-line takeaway

## 3. Solution Section

### Section Header
Beacon turns live inquiry into discovery

### Short explanation
When you have a valuable conversation with your LLM, Beacon turns it into a structured inquiry you can save, revisit, and optionally open up to matching.

### 3-step flow
1. **Explore**  
   Go deep on an idea in your LLM of choice

2. **Beacon it**  
   Turn the conversation into a clean, structured Beacon

3. **Discover**  
   See who else is on the same question — or who has the missing perspective

## 9. FAQ Section

### Q1. Is Beacon another chatbot?
No. Beacon sits on top of your existing LLM workflow and helps you act on the best ideas that come out of it.

### Q2. Do I need a big audience?
No. Beacon is designed to help you find relevant people even if you have zero followers.

### Q3. Does Beacon share all my chats?
No. Only the conversations you explicitly Beacon are turned into inquiry objects.

### Q4. Who is this for?
People who use LLMs as thought partners: founders, researchers, engineers, and curious power users.
